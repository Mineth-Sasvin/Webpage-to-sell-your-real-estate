TRUEMANS - **Guide price £1,000,000 - £1,150,000** This exclusive new development is quietly hidden away on a quirky one- way street right in the cultural heart of the city, WITH OFF STREET PARKING! Whilst the outside of this house looks very traditional, a clue to the contemporary delights within are the energy efficient windows, keeping the house airy in the summer but warm and welcoming during winter, too.

Stepping into a glamorous lifestyle, the ground floor has an immediate visual impact. Partly opened to views over the room below, there’s a long window along the far wall and an internal wall of glass across the stylish lounge which is full of character with exposed brick arches and a flame effect gas ‘wood burner’.

Downstairs, the luxurious kitchen diner spans the full depth of the building with plenty of room to relax or entertain and with the double height ceiling over the comfortable seating area, it feels beautifully open and restful. At the far end, the streamlined Italian kitchen is tucked away behind an impressive central island which stretches a full 3 metres, so there’s ample space for friends to join you. All the integrated appliances are high spec, and the expert design continues in the smooth, stone surfaces.

Up on the first floor, the two generous double bedrooms were designed to impress. Each have en suite shower rooms with glamorous walk in showers which have rain fall heads, and there’s a contemporary bathroom which also has high end Villeroy and Boch fittings. Private on the top floor, the stunning master bedroom is all about relaxing, and its crowning glory is a private roof terrace with oblique sea views, perfect for breakfast or for quiet nightcaps.

Steine Street is ideal for commuters, trendsetters and investors as this exceptional house is in a sought-after location within minutes of fashionable shopping, restaurants, clubs, cinema theatres and Brighton beach. Brighton Station is also within easy reach with direct access into London!

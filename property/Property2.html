Discreetly tucked away in a tranquil and secluded position adjoining the prestigious South Winchester Golf Course is this exquisite and wonderfully spacious converted barn with accommodation approaching 7,000 sq. ft including the garage complex.

PROPERTY
Rarely available within Pitt hamlet, this character barn conversion is blended with a contemporary, yet traditional twist. Elegantly finished to the highest quality having been project managed by a renowned local architect and found in the most unique of locations.

THE BARN
Walking into this family home you are greeted with a magnificent reception room with a 30ft vaulted ceiling and exposed beams. The galleried landing is exquisite and provides a link between two sides of the property and floor-to-ceiling windows and doors at either end of this space drench the reception room in natural light. There are wonderful views of the garden and outside making this one of many perfect places to unwind, entertain and showcase to your guests. This space is an obvious heartbeat of the home and leads through to a sitting room and onwards to the modern, yet traditional, open plan kitchen/dining room with garden aspects. Designed with entertaining in mind, it has an impressive central island with ample storage space, a beautiful Aga and there is a rather quirky hidden cellar underneath the kitchen which is accessed via stairs, perfect for someone to use as a wine cellar. There is also a very useful utility room. Across the other side of the reception sitting room and into an inner hallway, four of the five bedrooms can be found, again with exposed beams and woodwork. Two of the bedrooms share an en-suite shower room and all are close to the family bathroom. An imposing staircase leads up to a grand family room with garden and golf course views. Truly one of a kind, this space is perfect to sit and relax and has a balcony area, ideal for watching the afternoon sun, overlooking the gardens to the south/east of the barn on one side. The galleried landing leads onto the wonderful principle bedroom with beautiful oak beams and large windows providing further natural light. There is also a dressing room and well-appointed en-suite bathroom.

ANNEXE
This substantial building is more than your typical annexe, with an impressive duo of rooms, a kitchen and gym/office and there is a cellar with two further rooms. This annex is truly a bonus to this property, where it could easily be used as a guest house for friends and family or even a teenager’s hideaway.

OUTSIDE
The property is located up a track off the main road and has a driveway that sweeps around the whole of the property. The Long Barn sits centrally within a plot of approximately half an acre of delightfully mature gardens which comprise of mature hedging, carefully cultivated lawns and colourful shrubs providing excellent levels of privacy. The garage has plenty of storage for several cars.

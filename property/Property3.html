9 Marine Drive occupies an elevated end costal plot, benefiting from exceptional uninterrupted westerly sea views across all three balconies. This is the perfect property for a holiday home, an investment purchase or family home.

The Village of Bigbury-on-Sea and Challaborough Bay are both renowned for their beautiful beaches, water sports, nearby 18 Hole Golf Course, wonderful coastal walks and the spectacular Burgh Island, which can be accessed on foot (tide permitting) or via the locally operated Sea Tractor. Sailing can be enjoyed in nearby Newton Ferrers or Salcombe both of which are only a short car journey away. Burgh Islands famed luxury art deco hotel and pub, The Pilchard both offering a great option for either fine dining or traditional pub fare are within easy walking distance. There is a good range of shopping and other amenities in the market town of Kingsbridge, whilst the Georgian town of Modbury has a wide selection of independent shops, pubs and eateries.

On Entering 59 Marine drive, instantly the bright and spacious accommodation showcases how thoughtfully designed this property is. Leading down the first staircase takes you onto the first floor where the family bathroom and two double bedrooms can be found. The front double bedroom occupying an en-suite and large decked balcony accessed through large bi-fold doors achieving sea views.

On the second Floor two further double bedrooms can be found both affording en-suites and spacious living accommodation. Off the front bedroom, a second balcony accessed through bi-fold doors is also presented creating a bright area which can be enjoyed all times of the day. From watching the early sunrises to sunsets from the comfort of your own bed, what more could you ask for. You really are living the dream.

Leading down the third staircase to the third floor the open plan kitchen/ living area showcases the grand living accommodation 59 Marine Drive has to offer. At the rear of the room the modern white fitted kitchen comprises of granite preparation area, integral appliances creating a very clean and workable kitchen. Off the sides of the room there is a utility room with space for a washing machine, a cloak room and separate wc and access to the side of the property.

The open plan living area with access to the large decked balcony accessed through two bi-folding doors creates a larger entertaining space, perfect for all the family to enjoy. The combination of the modern touches and wooden finishes has been thoughtfully designed and executed to a high standard, making this property the perfect home.

About Us

Our Business Model

We are the UK’s number one property portal and the UK’s largest property marketplace. We bring the UK’s largest and most engaged property audience and the largest inventory of properties together in one place. We benefit from strong network effects as our property audience and the properties our customers advertise create a ‘virtuous circle’ enhancing the our value proposition.The our network effect
